About Us

MFMCF

MFMCF stands for Mountain Of Fire And Miracles Ministry Campus Fellowship,a Christain student organization on university campuses. Mountain Of Fire And Miracle Ministries (MFM) is a pentecostal christain organization founded in Nigeria, and it's campus fellowship aims to spread the gospel,support spiritual growth,and provide a community for christain students on campus.

MFMCF provides a platform for students to come together ,worship,and grow in their faith. It is a wonderful way to stay connected with your spiritual roots while pursuing your academic goals.May God continue to guide and bless you as you navigate through your academic journey!
